Recruitment

Teaching Staff

A candidate who:



Aspires to make a career in teaching

Has command over subject matter

Has good command over spoken English

Has a winning attitude and appetite to face challenge

Has sincerity in actions and commitment in outlook

Needs an honest, transparent and supportive work culture which promotes talent


Specific Requirement:Expertise in guiding students for exams like JEE (Main/Advanced) and other State Engineering Entrance Exams.

Desired Profiles (for Faculty Positions):

A M.Sc. (Physics / Chemistry / Mathematics) candidate graduated recently and is looking for opportunities Engineering Entrance Exams training arena. Candidates already teaching in the training industry and has good success rate.

OR

Expert teachers in Physics / Chemistry / Mathematics working at any other training institute for preparing students for IIT-JEE and is keen to work in highly motivated & systematic environment.

OR

A B.Tech graduate from IITs / NITs (Formerly REC), looking for opportunities in the highly attractive & competitive IIT-JEE training field.

OR

Faculty members at IITs / reputed Colleges / Universities, who are keen to work in a highly motivated & organized environment of Engineering Entrance training.

OR

Secondary School teachers who are keen to work in a highly motivated & higher-growth-oriented environment of IIT-JEE training.
